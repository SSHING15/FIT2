Global Food Supply

INFORMTION about MAP

The food price index represents the price of food in each respective country. The world average food price index level is 100, with a higher value representing more expensive food prices relative to the world. South Korea takes the number one spot with a food index price of 202.794, double the global average. Australia has a higher average food price with a global ranking of 14 and a food price index of 153.54.

INFORMTION about LINE

As of 2018 the United States of America has the highest daily caloric supply on the globe which is reflected in in their obesity levels with over 41.9% of adults in Americans being obese according to 2017-2020 National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey. On the other hand 1 in 3 children in Zimbabwe suffer from malnutrition, reflected by the very low daily caloric supply in the graph. The steep rise in China’s caloric supply highlights China’s 20% increase in abdominal obesity from 1993 to 2015 according to Hemmingsson.
